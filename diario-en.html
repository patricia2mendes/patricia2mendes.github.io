Diário de Notícias da Madeira

My first professional experience

My first professional experience in management was at Diário de Notícias da Madeira, in the Marketing and Events Department.

DIÁRIO organises more than 15 events a year, which I've had the opportunity to collaborate on. These events range from shows to conferences and other premium events.

There, I created value propositions and negotiated with sponsors, budgeted with suppliers and devised marketing strategies adapted to the timings and the various events. What's more, as this is an influential media outlet on the island of Madeira, I made the most of the experience by networking. I discovered an inescapable passion for radio and fuelled my love of writing. I ended up writing some articles and helping with news content, and I wrote and recorded several spots for TSF Madeira, as well as voicing some sponsored spots for clients.
